About Kulldeep

Background

Born on April 9, 1978, in Punjab, India, Kulldeep Sandhu has established himself as a versatile and prolific artist in the Indian music industry.

With decades of experience, he has shaped the soundscapes of countless videos, shorts, and television series, proving his mastery over rhythm and melody.

Musical Expertise

Genres: Punjabi folk, Indian classical, Devotional

Occupations: Composer, Singer, Music Producer, Writer, Actor

His extensive catalog features hundreds of credits ranging from devotional tracks like "Shri Hanuman Chalisa" to modern music videos and film compositions.
